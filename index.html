Statues


Statues is a sci-fi dystopian YA novel that I'm writing in my free time. Set in Manchester in the 2070s, Statues follows the journey of a trans-fem teenager named Robin who is forced to run jobs for the local gang as repayment for them taking her in. She's tired of spending every day of her life on the run and just wants to live a normal life but when her last friend gets taken away from her she is forced to turn to the offensive.
